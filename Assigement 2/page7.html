VR Language Exchange Platform

Introduction:
Welcome to VR Language Exchange! We value your privacy and aim to create a safe environment where language enthusiasts from around the world can come together to learn and grow. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect user information.
Scope:
This policy applies to all interactions within the VR Language Exchange community, including the VR app, website, and any associated features.
Personal Information:
When signing up for VR Language Exchange, you may be asked to provide certain personal information, such as your full name, email address, age, gender, and country of residence. You may choose to add additional details to your profile at your discretion. All personal information collected is kept strictly confidential and used solely for the purposes described in this policy.
Communication:
VR Language Exchange uses secure messaging tools to facilitate communication among members. Messages sent through our system are end-to-end encrypted to protect your privacy. Users are responsible for keeping their messages private outside of the VR Language Exchange platform.
Content Sharing:
Users may opt to share content, such as audio recordings, text conversations, images, videos, and other materials, during their language exchanges. By sharing content, you grant VR Language Exchange a non-exclusive license to display, reproduce, modify, adapt, publish, translate, create derivative works from, distribute, and perform such content worldwide, in perpetuity, for the purposes of operating and promoting VR Language Exchange.
Data Storage and Security:
All personal information collected by VR Language Exchange is stored securely and accessibly only to authorized personnel. Our servers utilize industry-standard security protocols to safeguard against unauthorized access, loss, misuse or alteration of user data.
Data Retention:
Your personal information will be retained only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes stated in this policy. When there is no longer a legitimate reason to retain your information it will be deleted or made anonymous.
Sharing Personal Information:
VR Language Exchange does not sell, rent or lease or otherwise disclose your personal information to third parties unless required by law or to enforce our Terms of Service. In rare instances we may share your information with trusted third-party vendors who assist us in operating VR Language Exchange provided those vendors agree to keep your information confidential.
